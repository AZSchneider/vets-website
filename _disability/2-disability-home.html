Disability compensation is a key benefit that helps Veterans

A disability can result from a disease or injury sustained, or aggravated, while a Veteran was on active duty - or appear after the Veteran’s period of active duty service.

Disability Compensation is generally linked to the loss of ability to work caused directly, or indirectly, by the disability.
