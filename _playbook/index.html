Building a single unified digital experience for all veterans.

The Challenge

  • 1000 websites
  • 956 1-800 numbers
  • 42 Call Centers
  • 220 Databases

Today, the VA customer experience is fragmented and confusing. Weâ€™re not setting clear expectations and the right information at the right time is hard to find.

Our customers want to know what to expect. They want access digestible information in language they understand, and they want tools that are easy to find and use.

The new Vets.gov is the answer this challenge.

Our Goals

  • Allow people to get their needs met on the first try interacting with Vets.Gov from any device.
  • Create a new mobile-first platform using agile and iterative processes.
  • Gradually redesign and design end-to-end experiences that are currently within existing applications. This will be a methodical process, done in partnership with business owners across VA
  • Provide transparency about our processes, to our customers
  • Dramatically improve the user experience of VA digital touchpoints, unifying and simplifying them for our customers

Design Principles

  1. Design for people not VA systems, understand human needs.
  2. Assume every visitor is new.
  3. Help people reach their goals, simply and smoothly, every time.
  4. Speak openly, respectfully and directly.
  5. Connect and create opportunities for feedback and dialogue.
  6. Research, observe, test, and continuously improve.
  7. Be device agnostic.
  8. Employ modern development practices, be agile
