Templates

The vets.gov template system is designed to help visitors easily move through information, identify whatâ€™s relevant to them and quickly take action.

Content Templates

Topic Landing Page

Topic landing pages introduce main site areas such as Disability Benefits. The template includes a brief introduction and card navigation linking to secondary content. It may also include quick links [Link] and help or contact information specific to the topic.

Detailed Landing Page

Detailed landing pages communicate an overview of secondary information with links to faceted content as is the case with Conditions linking to specific condition types. The template utilizes list style navigation and may include callout boxes, quick links and help or contact information specific to the topic.

Panel Filter Page

Panel filter pages help visitors quickly identify a content paths that EW relevant to their specific circumstances. The template includes definition blocks that function as a filtering mechanism.

Action Page

Action pages provide in-depth detail on particular topics and should help visitors to feel confident in taking next steps or accessing calls-to-action as needed. The template may also include definition blocks, Callout boxes and help or contact information specific to the topic.

Info Page

Info pages are basic content page and include an introduction and supporting body copy.

Process Page

Process pages support visitors through complex workflows and set them up for successful engagement with VA process. The template utilizes a process list and may also include list form navigation.
