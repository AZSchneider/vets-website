The Platform

The Vets.gov platform ensures both customers, and those building experiences for them, have a consistent experiences. It holds to high standards, utilizing the best in design research and technology.

Services and Infrastructure

Our platform provides central services, including:

  • Secure accounts
  • Robust analytics
  • Visual design system
  • UX Pattern System

These applications (apps) are well scoped and grouped by category. Using employment as an example, you’ll find apps for searching for jobs, posting a job listing, and translating military service into civilian skills.

Apps are in development, created by internal teams, contractors, or others, such as the VERC or 18F. All will benefit from our platform’s infrastructure in delivering improved functionality to customers more quickly. They’ll be able to do so without sacrificing quality, security, or privacy. New apps may be designed and sponsored by a business office, a VA administration, or in certain cases by the VE and/or DSE team. Our platform was built and is maintained internally within VA. It’s run by the VA Digital Service.

Standards

To be included in our platform, apps must meet rigorous standards. They need to be 508-compliant and adhere to automated test and code review standards. In some cases, a legacy system will simply not make the cut.

Levels of Complexity

The Vets.gov platform will include features ranging from basic information to personalized applications. Here are the range of offerings and the architecture that supports them.

  • Level 1: Clear Information
    Features: None
    Hosting: Github
    FISMA: N/A
    Example: New Disability Claims content
  • Level 2: Basic Tools
    Features: Interactive forms
    Hosting: Amazon web services pilot (GSA IAA)
    FISMA: Low
    Example: Saved Veterans Employment Center information
  • Level 3: Account Linked Tools
    Features: Personalization
    Hosting: Amazon web services pilot (GSA IAA)
    FISMA: Moderate
    Example: Saved Veterans Employment Center information
  • Level 4: Sensitive Data Linked Tools
    Features: Integrated User Profile
    Hosting: TBD
    FISMA: Moderate
